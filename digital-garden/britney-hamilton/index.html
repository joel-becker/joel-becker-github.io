An example of how one might arrange one of my mashups.

Some notes on this one:

  1. The arrangement is very simple and rigid. Ideally you want to get some confident singers singing lyrics to different songs over one another, flowing in and out of songs as feels right. But the simpler way to do this – wordless melody, as here – works nicely, especially with a less familiar crowd.
  2. The worldess melody here is from Hamilton’s “My Shot.”
  3. Another feature that makes this easier to follow: matching rapped lyrics with sung melodies.
  4. You can mix this Havana, Levitating, or Hotel California. (If adding Hotel California, changing around the root on the Bm and D especially can be helpful; the original goes roughly Bm, F#, A, E; G, D, Em, F#.)
