Joel Becker

I work at FTX Foundation, on leave from an economics Ph.D. at NYU.

Previously, I worked as a Predoctoral Fellow at Harvard University and completed a BSc in Economics and Econometrics at the University of Bristol. I also co-owned a statistics consultancy advising professional football teams, hosted a social media presence peaking at 300,000 followers, and was the inaugural chair of the UK's largest Young Labour constituency organisation. In spare time I enjoy a good sing-along and keeping healthy.
