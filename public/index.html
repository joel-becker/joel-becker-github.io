Joel Becker

I am currently building bespoke quantitative models to support strategic decision-making (e.g. cost-effectiveness models) at AI safety and biosecurity organizations (on a consulting basis -- contact me!). I am also grantmaking through Manifund.

Previously, I worked at a stealth AI safety organization, various nascent civilizational resilience organizations that came out of a conference I organized, FTX Foundation, the Global Priorities Institute, Harvard University, and the Institute for Fiscal Studies. I was an economics graduate student at New York University and an undergraduate at the University of Bristol.

I also co-owned a statistics consultancy advising professional football teams, hosted a social media presence peaking at 300,000 followers, and was the inaugural chair of the UK's largest Young Labour constituency organisation. In spare time I enjoy a good sing-along and keeping physically and financially healthy.
